The neural crest is a mesenchymal cell population that arises alongside the neural tube in vertebrate embryos. Neural crest cells then migrate throughout the embryo and become many different cell types.

As a PhD student I characterised the localisation and function of Pard3 in neural crest cells cells. I showed that microtubule dynamics are different at different subcellular locations.
